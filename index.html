BUTTON LAB — TOUCHLESS UI

“To drive innovation, never stop exploring new ways and possibilities of interacting with stuff.” — This is the Button Lab. Made for very fast prototyping touchless buttons on displays in specific situations.

Watch the video of the functional prototype on vimeo

BROSE — IAA INNOVATION STUDY

In 2015 we worked together with Brose to create an animated movie to present the latest Brose engineering innovations. This innovations were built into one vehicle, designed by Julius Schlecht. After placing this vehicle into a lab-like scenery and creating a storyboard to fly over every innovative Brose part, we produced a 2 minute animation, which was shown at IAA 2015.

More information on the Brose website.
